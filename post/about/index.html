😎 我的骄傲/What I am proud of...

✒️我组织编写了2015版吉林大学飞跃手册，截至2020年这仍然是吉林大学最优秀最全面的飞跃手册；
I conducted writing the Jilin University Applybook 3ed (a book that helps Chinese students to prepare for standarlized tests and to apply to graduate institutions abroad);

⌨️我曾担任中国最大的留学论坛寄托天下语言备考区和留学申请区版主，义务帮助素未蒙面的同学准备出国留学申请；
For over three years, I served as a BBS moderator for gter.net, the largest non-profit organization in China helping domestic students to apply to foreign schools;

🔑在我读本科期间，我协助组织吉林大学跨学科沙龙。这是一个为各学院喜欢分享的同学创办的一个地下组织，聚是一团火，散是满天星，沙龙的同学们现在遍布世界各地；
During my undergraduate study, I helped to organize the Interdisciplinary Salon (a dead-poet-society-like unregistered group, pretty cool lul). This group is to help students across colleges to exchange thoughts and ideas and to foster research collaborations;

🖥我是GraphiCon的创办者之一，这是目前中国最大的图形学和交互技术交流论坛GAMES的前身.
I helped to start GraphiCon, a discussion group lately turns into GAMES (Chinese largest research forum for computer graphics and interactive technology research).
